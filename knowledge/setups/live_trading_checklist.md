# Live Trading Checklist

## Pre-Trade Analysis

### 1. Session Context
- [ ] What session? (Asia, London, NY AM, NY PM)
- [ ] Any major news events today?
- [ ] What is the daily/weekly bias?

### 2. Key Levels (Mark on Chart)
- [ ] Previous Day High (PDH) - Buy-side liquidity
- [ ] Previous Day Low (PDL) - Sell-side liquidity
- [ ] Session High/Low
- [ ] Equal Highs/Lows (liquidity pools)
- [ ] MA20, MA50, MA200 levels
- [ ] Any unfilled FVGs from higher timeframes

### 3. Current Structure
- [ ] Where is price relative to MAs?
- [ ] Recent swing highs/lows identified?
- [ ] Any liquidity pools nearby?

---

## Entry Checklist (SMC Reversal)

### Before Entry - ALL Must Be True:
- [ ] Liquidity has been raided (wick through key level)
- [ ] Displacement candles present (strong directional move)
- [ ] Break of Structure CONFIRMED (candle CLOSED through swing)
- [ ] Price has retraced to FVG zone
- [ ] Order Block confluence present (ideal, not required)
- [ ] Risk:Reward is minimum 1:1.5 (prefer 1:2+)

### Entry Execution:
- [ ] Entry price identified
- [ ] Stop loss placed (beyond liquidity raid)
- [ ] Take profit at opposing liquidity
- [ ] Position size appropriate (max 2% risk)

---

## Trade Management

### While In Trade:
- [ ] Monitor for signs of reversal against position
- [ ] Watch for new displacement against your trade
- [ ] Consider partial profits at 1:1 R:R
- [ ] Move stop to breakeven after 1:1 reached

### Exit Signals:
- [ ] Target hit (opposing liquidity)
- [ ] New BOS against your position
- [ ] Strong displacement against your trade
- [ ] Time stop (trade not working after X bars)

---

## Post-Trade Review

- [ ] Did I follow the checklist?
- [ ] Was the setup valid per SMC rules?
- [ ] What worked / what didn't?
- [ ] Screenshot the trade for review

---

## Quick Signals Reference

### STRONG SHORT Signals:
- Buy-side liquidity raided (highs swept)
- Red displacement candles
- Close below swing low
- Rejection at MA200 or session high

### STRONG LONG Signals:
- Sell-side liquidity raided (lows swept)
- Green displacement candles
- Close above swing high
- Bounce at MA50/MA200 or session low

### AVOID Trading When:
- No clear liquidity raid
- Choppy, overlapping candles (no displacement)
- Price stuck between MAs (consolidation)
- Major news pending
- End-of-day (last 5 mins of RTH)
